OUR MISSION

DRR Government Polytechnic, Davangere, provides quality based technical /professional three year diploma courses to the eligible students to meet the changing needs of the Industry, Business and Community. It develops skill, self confidence, courage, innovativeness and makes the students succeed as good citizens. It will also create environmental awareness among people. A set of qualified and dedicated staff who are responsive to changes with supportive infrastructure will provide training in a healthy environment. This institute provides an opportunity and leadership in continuing education to all aspirants by way of need based formal and informal education at reasonable charges. It will also strive for optimum use of all internal resources to generate revenue for institutional development. It also serves the rural community through community polytechnic scheme to impart technology in rural areas and to improve their standard of living. It promotes leadership and personality development in women."
